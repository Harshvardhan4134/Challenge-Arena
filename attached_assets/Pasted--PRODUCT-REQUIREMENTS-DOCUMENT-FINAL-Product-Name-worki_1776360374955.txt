🧠 Product Name (working)

Challenge Arena

🎯 1. Vision

Build a real-time challenge and matchmaking platform where players can create custom matches, form teams, compete, and build reputation.

Focus:

Fast matchmaking
Custom rules
Competitive identity
🎮 2. Target Product (V1)

Game:
Garena Free Fire

Platform:

Web (mobile-first)

Players struggle with:

relying on messy WhatsApp/Discord groups

“Create or join custom Free Fire matches instantly — no chaos, no waiting.”
